SWOT Analysis

Strategic assessment of the current market position
Strengths
  • Proven Founders: Deep expertise in e-commerce, logistics and scaling via ex-Razor Group track record.
  • $12M Seed Funding: Led by Lakestar — strong financial runway and instant enterprise credibility.
  • Zero-Migration Setup: Sits on top of SAP/Oracle as a "System of Action" — no ERP replacement needed, lowering sales barriers.
Weaknesses
  • Early Stage: Technology and operational stability still need to prove themselves at enterprise scale.
  • The Trust Gap: Handing financial decisions to autonomous AI carries high psychological barriers and hallucination risks.
  • Split Operations: Dual-hub setup between Berlin and Bangalore risks friction in early product cycles.
Opportunities
  • Massive Untapped Market: Global supply chains still run on unstructured emails, PDFs, and manual Excel sheets.
  • Labor Shortage: Severe back-office talent crunch accelerates enterprise demand for automation.
  • VC Network: Lakestar and Leo Capital provide warm introductions to DAX, MDAX, and global enterprises.
Threats
  • Fierce Competition: Incumbent giants (SAP, Celonis) and cash-rich US AI startups entering the supply chain space.
  • Big Tech Commoditisation: OpenAI/Anthropic releasing native workflow agents could bypass specialised layers.
  • Long Sales Cycles: Slow B2B procurement in Europe risks draining capital before mass adoption.
